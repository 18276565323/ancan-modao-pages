Quality Assurance

Original authentic components, strict inspection, and traceable delivery.

1

Channel Control

We screen suppliers strictly and source from factories and authorized channels whenever possible to reduce counterfeit risk at the source.

2

Incoming Inspection

Our QC workflow checks appearance, markings, labels, packaging, and required functional items.

3

Warehouse Management

Constant-temperature, humidity-controlled, anti-static warehousing follows ESD requirements to keep components in proper condition.

4

Shipment Traceability

Each shipment is managed by batch number to support traceability from procurement through delivery.

Three-Level Quality Inspection System

01
Level 1: Appearance and Packaging Inspection
Check package integrity, label consistency, lot number matching, and desiccant condition.
02
Level 2: Microscope Inspection
Inspect pin condition, body marking, sanding traces, and recoating indicators under magnification.
03
Level 3: Functional and Material Analysis
Conduct decap, X-ray, RoHS, or functional verification when required by the order.
Q

Every component matters to product stability. AnCan is committed to original authentic supply, responsible quality handling, and responsive after-sales support.
