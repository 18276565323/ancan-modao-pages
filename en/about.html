About Us

Company Profile

AnCan Electronics Co., Ltd. was founded in 2014 and is headquartered in Shenzhen, the core hub of China's electronics industry. We are a professional electronic component distributor committed to providing high-quality components and efficient supply chain solutions for OEM, ODM, and EMS customers worldwide.

After years of steady development, AnCan has built long-term cooperation with international semiconductor manufacturers and channel partners. Our products are widely used in industrial control, automotive electronics, communications, consumer electronics, and medical equipment.
